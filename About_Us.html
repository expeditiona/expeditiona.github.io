About Us

ExpeditioNA aims to ease the stress of planning a road trip. Our innovative website is powered by elegant CSS styling, JavaScript, modified Bootstrap, Firebase, and jQuery. With our powerful website, traveling will never be the same again.

We offer an assortment of different features to satisfy all your traveling needs from start to finish. Once you have booked your trip, it is time to start packing. What better way to organize the things you need to pack than to use our Checklist service? Its simple, interactive design makes even the most daunting lists easy to manipulate and use.

Once you are done packing, it is time to get out on the road. Powered by Google Maps API, our Route Planner service takes Google Maps one step further by indicating suggested rest stop locations along your journey. It maps out your trip and provides easy-to-follow directions that ensure that you reach your final destination.

To ensure that you maximize your stay, the POI service gives a variety of attractions and place to visit around the area. For those with food allergies, we've added an Allergen Info feature that helps you determine which restaurants can accommodate for your dietary needs. The Firebase database that powers this service is constantly expanding, with new restaurants and foods being added for the future.

Here at ExpeditioNA, our main goal is for you to have an enjoyable, stress-free traveling experience.
